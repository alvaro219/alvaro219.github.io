DIVERS:

Experts at initiating or finishing fights. These fighters are trained to be precise and forceful, so that they can do as much damage as possible before their enemies become a problem or finish off opponents who are reluctant to succumb.

CONTROLLERS:

Consistent and meticulous. Controllers are ingenious when it comes to dealing constant amounts of damage over a long period of time. Their favourite targets are big guys with huge amounts of health.

FIGHTERS:

Solitary and fierce. Fighters are famous not for their cooperative abilities but for presenting more of a threat on their own. They are resourceful in keeping themselves alive or undercutting the threat of their opponents.

TANKS:

Tough and protective. These incredible allies have excellent ways of keeping the team safe from enemies with either their shields or their own bodies. In any case, when a massive attack approaches your party, tanks are your best companions.

CONJURATORS

Balanced and varied. Conjurers are famous for their powerful allies. Summoners with immense and diverse abilities to help the party either by dealing damage, supporting the team or using enemies to their advantage.

HEALERS:

Vital and persistent. When all security measures fail and your team is at death's door, these excellent allies are ready to give you a second chance, or as many as it takes.

EXALTERS:

Dangerous and inspiring. While Exalters can lead to some undesirable outcomes, they are often great sources of support for the team. While they do not always control their full potential, they can be the difference between life and death in the most literal way possible.

MYSTICS:

Absolutes and manipulators. Mystics prefer to highlight their potential in abrupt ways. There is no middle ground with them, it's all or nothing. They are the most vivid representation of chance and can turn some fights into mere trivialities.
