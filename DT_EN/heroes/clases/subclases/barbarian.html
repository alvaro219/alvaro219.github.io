BARBARIAN
Role:Fighter.
Description:These fiendish warriors will fight to the bitter end, preferring to do things on their own. On rare occasions, though, they may end up relying on others.

INITIAL SKILLS

  • Pasive:When the Barbarian knocks down an enemy, he heals 1 health.
  • Skill:2 damage [Self-healing].
  • Rearguard:1 damage to a random enemy and 1 shield to a random ally.

HEROIC EVOLUTIONS

  • Legislator:"All right, I'll trust you. Don't make me regret it." Now, each time he knocks down an enemy he applies [Growth] 1 to a random ally and himself.
  • Warlord:"It's as if they grow with every victory they win". Now, every time he knocks down an enemy, [Vitality] 1 is applied to him.
