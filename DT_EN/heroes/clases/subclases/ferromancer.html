FERROMANCER
Role:Tank.
Description:Despite their cold, hard appearance, inside lurk normally small and vulnerable metal wizards. It is their marvellous ability to shape metal that makes them so amazing.

INITIAL SKILLS

  • Pasive:The Ferromancer receives 2 shield at the start of each turn that does not go away at the end of the turn.
  • Skill:4 shield [Pain].
  • Rearguard:2 shield to the ally who will take the most damage.

HEROIC EVOLUTIONS

  • Guardian:"I never thought that a badly cut piece of sheet metal could deflect a bullet. Now the shield left over at the end of the turn is distributed to all allies until the end of the next turn.
  • Colossus:"What are enemy shields for if I can't use them as armour later?". Now whenever he would lose life to [Pain] it is applied to his shield instead of his life and he doesn't lose any shield at the end of the turn.
