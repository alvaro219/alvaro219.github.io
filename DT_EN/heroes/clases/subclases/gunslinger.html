GUNSLINGER
Role:Controller.
Description:The Pistoleros have one thing clear, one gun is better than a bow, but two are better than one, although a rocket launcher would be the bomb... but... and two rocket launchers...?

INITIAL SKILLS

  • Pasive:At the start of your turn you turn the barrel of your revolver which consists of 1 incendiary bullet, 1 explosive bullet and 4 normal bullets. Incendiary bullets cause [Penetration] to the skill you perform, explosive bullets cause 1 damage to all allies if you used a skill or 1 damage to all enemies if you used an attack, and normal bullets do not modify anything.
  • Skill:5 damage [Cooldown].
  • Rearguard:1 damage to the enemy with the most health.

HEROIC EVOLUTIONS

  • Duelist:"Turiruriru (dry bush rolling) wua wua wua wuaaa". Its abilities now apply [Growth] if it targets the previous turn's enemy.
  • Captain:Changes the 4 normal bullets in his magazine to 2 incendiary and 2 explosive bullets. His incendiary bullets now also deal damage to shields, excess shield damage is applied as normal damage, and explosive bullets will always deal 1 damage to enemies.
