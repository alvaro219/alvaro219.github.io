LANCER
Role:Diver.
Description:No cheating, no help, one on one. These brave warriors, armed with courage, push their limits day after day, proving that despite falling, they can always get up again.

INITIAL SKILLS

  • Pasive:The Lancer's abilities have a 33% chance to deal 1 more damage to his targets if they are at or above his health.
  • Skill:2 damage [Initiate].
  • Rearguard:1 damage to enemy with less health.

HEROIC EVOLUTIONS

  • Hoplite:"Woe betide anyone who meets a Hoplite head-on. Their abilities now gain [Double] if their targets have health above 75% of their maximum health.
  • Dragon Knight:Completely removes the ability to deal 1 extra damage. Instead, he will always delay his action by 1 turn if he performs an ability, he will get [Jump] for the rest of the turn and his action will get [Double].
