WIZARD
Role:Controller.
Description:Though sometimes misunderstood, these beings threaten to wield a level of power beyond the reach of few minds. Whether it's wiping out a large group of goblins or a huge troll, no one will complain about having one of these at their side.

INITIAL SKILLS

  • Pasive:Mage abilities with [Spread] have a 33% chance to deal 1 extra damage to the target of the ability.
  • Skill:1 damage [Spread].
  • Rearguard:1 damage to the enemy with the most health.

HEROIC EVOLUTIONS

  • Red Mage:Abilities with [Spread] now have a 25% chance to deal 1 extra damage to any wounded enemy with that ability and a 25% chance to heal 1 health to a random ally.
  • Star Mage:"Stars don't lie, they reveal." Abilities with [Scatter] now have a 50% chance to deal 1 bonus damage to any enemy wounded with that ability.
