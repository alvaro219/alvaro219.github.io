CROUPIER
Role:Mystic.
Description:Croupiers are lovers of randomness and will accept any fate that is written on their cards. Even if it means a terrible end...

INITIAL SKILLS

  • Pasive:At the start of your turn you draw 1 card from a 10-card deck with 3 red, 3 green and 4 grey cards. The red cards subtract 1 from the skill you perform, the green cards add 1 and the grey cards do nothing.
  • Skill:-1 to an enemy [Petrify].
  • Rearguard:-1 to a random enemy.

HEROIC EVOLUTIONS

  • Enchanter:"Come closer without fear, I don't bite! Although my cards sometimes do...". Remove all green and red cards and add 2 blue and 4 grey cards. The blue cards can apply [Poison] or [Spread].
  • Cardmancer:"What did you get? Well, nothing a pair of teeth can't pay for!". Swap the grey cards for yellow. Yellow cards can apply [Poison], [Regeneration], [Growth], or [Decay].
