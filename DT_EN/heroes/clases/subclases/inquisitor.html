INQUISITOR
Role:Fighter.
Description:These soldiers of the faith are exposed to a lot of pressure, many begin the path of righteousness and few succeed in finishing it. Some as heroes, others... simply achieve their goals.

INITIAL SKILLS

  • Pasive:If an enemy wounds the Inquisitor, he gains +1 against such enemies until the end of the next turn.
  • Skill:2 damage [Duel].
  • Rearguard:1 damage to a random enemy and 1 shield to a random ally.

HEROIC EVOLUTIONS

  • Blessed Sword:"Those who feel lonely can find the best families in their companions. All allies now benefit from +1 against such enemies and are healed by 1 when dealing damage to them.
  • Cursed Sword:"Creature, can't you see you are an aberration that I will correct?". Now when you wound enemies that wound you, they get -1 and the accumulation won't be until the end of the turn.
