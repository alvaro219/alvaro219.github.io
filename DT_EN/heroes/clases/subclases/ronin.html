RONIN
Role:Controller.
Description:Ronin are lovers of the art of swordsmanship. They train day in and day out with their techniques to craft the perfect swordsman, even if it is dishonourable to some.

INITIAL SKILLS

  • Pasive:When the Ronin takes damage, there is a 25% chance of taking no damage and making an attack on the target that attacked him.
  • Skill:1 damage [Trigger].
  • Rearguard:1 damage to the enemy with the most health.

HEROIC EVOLUTIONS

  • Kyoshin:"Be prepared, if you see him sheathe his sword, it will be too late." Now when he successfully knocks down an enemy with an ability with [Chaining], he deals 1 damage to the enemy with the most health.
  • Katsujinken:"He wields the sword as if it were an extension of his arm. Now, whenever he avoids taking damage, he makes an attack on all enemies instead of just one.
