THIEF
Role:Diver.
Description:Thieves are reviled by society and rightly so. Deceitful, treacherous or cruel can be perfect nicknames for these masters of the shadows. Although sometimes they may have noble causes.

INITIAL SKILLS

  • Pasive:Thief Skills have a 25% chance of executing targets that have life equal to 25% of their maximum life or less.
  • Skill:2 damage [Cruel].
  • Rearguard:1 damage to enemy with less health.

HEROIC EVOLUTIONS

  • Shadow:"These masters of the shadows have perfected the ancient art of slashing." Now whenever he executes an enemy, he gains [Stealth] for that turn.
  • Spy:"They have learned to use alternative, but no less effective, methods". Their abilities now have a 50% chance to deal 1 extra damage to enemies below 50% of their maximum health.
