BARD
Role:Exalter.
Description:Bards are insecure souls who may feel blocked or mentally inspired, something that can change their performance in the heat of battle.

INITIAL SKILLS

  • Pasive:Bard skills have a 33% chance of costing twice as much energy or no energy at all.
  • Skill:+1 to an ally [Spread].
  • Rearguard:+1 to a random ally.

HEROIC EVOLUTIONS

  • Pictomancer:"Take an alternative, safer, more accurate artistic route. Loses the chance to perform a skill for double energy or free, but has a 10% chance to apply the performed skill twice.
  • Trapezist:"He accepts his mistakes in order to shine more brightly when he does something spectacular, criticising himself more severely. Your skills may now cost twice as much energy and not be performed or be performed for free and cast twice.
